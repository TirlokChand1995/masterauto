Company History and its Operation


It gives me great pleasure to introduces you to Master auto Components which was established in the year 2007 as a manufacturer of Precision Machined & turned Components and sheet metal components. The Company has started Component manufacturing in the year April 2007.We are committed for tough challenges in the form of stringent specifications & rigorous quality standards set by valuable customers – this has been confronted by dedication, commitment and excellence in work.

Today’s MASTER AUTO COMPONENTS is manufacturing Precision machined & turned components with MS. EN1A ,EN3A etc.We have tie-up with various close associates for value additions on the components i.e powder Coating , Electro Plating Hardening & tempering etc.

We, the team at MASTER AUTO COMPONENTS. function in perfect synchronization with the customer and are capable of achieving highest quality standards set by the appreciates over the year and a perfect product is the bond for such continued relationship.

We assure you of our best services & commitment with high degree of quality & reliability in the products, and anticipate our association with your company.
We manufacture critical components for automobiles and machine parts . Our company is sure to pursue the new products & technology and, meanwhile, approach towards our goal “To keep quality up and get the cost down”.
